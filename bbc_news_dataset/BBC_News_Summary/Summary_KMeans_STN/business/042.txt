UK Coal plunges into deeper lossShares in UK Coal have fallen after the mining group reported losses had deepened to £51.6m in 2004 from £1.2m. In early trade on Thursday, its shares were down 10% at 119 pence. It expected 2005 to be a "transitional year" and to return to profitability in 2006. "We have a long journey ahead to fix these issues. UK Coal operates 15 deep and surface mines across Nottinghamshire, Derbyshire, Leicestershire, Yorkshire, the West Midlands, Northumberland and Durham.
