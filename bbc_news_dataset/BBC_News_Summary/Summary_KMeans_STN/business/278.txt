SBC currently employs 163,000 people while AT&T employs 47,000. AT&T is a long-distance telecoms firm, while SBC is mainly focused on the local market in the western US. The companies said they expected to complete the agreement during the first half of 2006. SBC and AT&T said estimated cost savings of at least $2bn from 2008 were a main driver for the merger. Both also have data network businesses.
