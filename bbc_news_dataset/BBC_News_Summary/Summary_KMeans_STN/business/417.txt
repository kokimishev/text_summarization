The two banks reportedly held talks in October and November 2004. Wells Fargo had net income of $6.2bn in its last financial year, a 9% increase on the previous year, and revenues of $28.4bn. UK banking icon Barclays was founded more than 300 years ago; it has operations in over 60 countries and employs 76,200 staff worldwide. Barclays has declined to comment on the report in the Sunday Express, saying it does not respond to market speculation. "The chief executive would be abandoning his duty if he did not talk to rivals, but a deal does not seem likely," Reuters quoted one trader as saying.
