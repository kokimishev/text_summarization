By 1700 GMT, Petrel's shares fell from 97p ($1.87) to 44p ($0.85). Petrel said that it has not received any information from Iraqi authorities to confirm or deny the report. Turkey's Everasia is reported by Reuters to have won a contract to develop the Khurmala Dome field in the north of the country. The winners of the contract are to build new flow lines and build gas separation stations. Oil officials hope to double Iraq's output by the end of the decade.
