Quiksilver moves for RossignolShares of Skis Rossignol, the world's largest ski-maker, have jumped as much as 15% on speculation that it will be bought by US surfwear firm Quiksilver. European sporting goods companies have seen foreign revenues squeezed by a slump in the value of the US dollar, making a takeover more attractive, analysts said. Rossignol shares touched 17.70 euros, before falling back to trade 7.8% higher at 16.60 euros.
