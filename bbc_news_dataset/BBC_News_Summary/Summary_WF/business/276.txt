 India power shares jump on debutShares in India's largest power producer, National Thermal Power Corp (NTPC) have risen 13% on their stock market debut. It was India's second $1bn stock debut in three months, coming after the flotation by software firm Tata. The share offer was eleven times oversubscribed. "Power needs in India are set to rise and NTPC will benefit from that." The firm is the largest utility company in India, and the sixth largest power producer in the world.
