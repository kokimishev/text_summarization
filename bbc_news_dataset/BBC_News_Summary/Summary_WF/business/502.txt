 Algeria hit by further gas riotsAlgeria suffered a weekend of violent protests against government plans to raise gas prices, local press reports. Demonstrators in a number of regions blocked roads, attacked public buildings and overturned vehicles, newspapers including El Watan reported. Even before the hike, failing economic conditions had been fanning resentment in some of Algeria's poorest regions. According to local press reports, trouble flared up again on Saturday and carried on into Sunday.
