 Huge rush for Jet Airways sharesIndian airline Jet Airways' initial public offering was oversubscribed 16.2 times, bankers said on Friday. Over 85% of the bids were at the higher end of the price range of 1,050-1,125 rupees ($24-$26). Jet Airways, a low-fare airline, was founded by London-based ex-travel agent Naresh Goya, and controls 45% of the Indian domestic airline market. The price at which its shares will begin trading will be agreed over the weekend, bankers said. "The demand for the IPO was impressive.
