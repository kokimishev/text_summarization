 EBay stock fell to $92.9 in after-hours trade, from a $103.05 end on Nasdaq. Excluding special items, eBay's profit was 33 cents a share, but analysts had expected 34 cents. said Janco Partners analyst Martin Pyykkonen. For 2004 as a whole, eBay earned $778.2m on sales of $3.27bn. Analysts had previously estimated that eBay would achieve 2005 revenues of $4.37bn and earnings of $1.62 per share, excluding items.
