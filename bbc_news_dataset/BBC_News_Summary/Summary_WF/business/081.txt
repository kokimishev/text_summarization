 BMW to recall faulty diesel carsBMW is to recall all cars equipped with a faulty diesel fuel-injection pump supplied by parts maker Robert Bosch. Bosch has stopped production of the part but expects to restart by 2 February. A spokesman for DaimlerChrysler, parent company of Mercedes Benz, said it will however have to halt some production. Audi said it had been hit by production bottlenecks, due to a shortage of unaffected Bosch parts.
