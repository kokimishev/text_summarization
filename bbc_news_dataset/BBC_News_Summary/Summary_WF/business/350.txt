 Dollar hits new low versus euroThe US dollar has continued its record-breaking slide and has tumbled to a new low against the euro. They have predicted that the dollar is likely to fall even further. The US currency was trading at $1.364 per euro at 1800 GMT on Monday. The dollar has weakened sharply since September when it traded about $1.20 against the euro. Traders said that thin trading levels had amplified Monday's move.
