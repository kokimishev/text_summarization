Philip Knight, chairman and chief executive, said: "Nike's second-quarter revenues and earnings per share reached all-time high levels as a result of solid performance across our global portfolio. Strong quarterly growth for NikeNike has reported its best second-quarter earnings, helped by strong demand for its athletic shoes and Converse sneakers. The firm reported worldwide futures orders for athletic footwear and gear, scheduled for delivery from December 2004 to April 2005, of $4.9bn. Revenues increased 11% to $3.1bn, from $2.8bn for the same period in 2003.
