On Wednesday, the German Federal Labour Agency said the jobless total had reached 5.037 million in January, which takes the jobless rate to 12.1%. Adjusted for the seasonal factors, the German jobless total rose by 227,000 in January from December. "Yes, we have effectively more than five million people unemployed," a government minister said earlier on ZDF public television. Five million Germans out of workGermany's unemployment figure rose above the psychologically important level of five million last month. Unemployment has not been this high in Germany since the 1930s.
