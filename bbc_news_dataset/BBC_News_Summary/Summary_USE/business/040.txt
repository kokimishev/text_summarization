Shares in Umbro were up 1.76% at 115.5 pence in morning trade. Umbro profits lifted by Euro 2004UK sportswear firm Umbro has posted a 222% rise in annual profit after sales of replica England football kits were boosted by the Euro 2004 tournament. In January, Umbro announced its sponsorship agreement with Chelsea, which gave Umbro the lucrative right to make replica shirts, would end in 2006, five years earlier than expected. Umbro, which recently lost sponsorship deals with Chelsea and Celtic, said on Thursday it had signed a new four-year agreement with Scottish club Rangers. Chief executive Peter McGuigan said the firm plans to grow sales both in the UK and internationally.
