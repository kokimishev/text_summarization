Quiksilver moves for RossignolShares of Skis Rossignol, the world's largest ski-maker, have jumped as much as 15% on speculation that it will be bought by US surfwear firm Quiksilver. Analysts believe other sporting goods companies may now take a closer look at Rossignol, prompting an auction and pushing the sale price higher. The owners of Rossignol, the Boix-Vives family, are said to be considering an offer from Quiksilver. Companies such as Quiksilver would be able to cut costs by selling Rossignol skis through their shops, they added.
