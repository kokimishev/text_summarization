Ethiopia's crop production up 24%Ethiopia produced 14.27 million tonnes of crops in 2004, 24% higher than in 2003 and 21% more than the average of the past five years, a report says. Agriculture is the main economic activity in Ethiopia, representing 45% of gross domestic product. In 2003, crop production totalled 11.49 million tonnes, the joint report from the Food and Agriculture Organisation and the World Food Programme said. About 80% of Ethiopians depend directly or indirectly on agriculture. The Food and Agriculture Organisation (FAO) recommend that the food assistance is bought locally.
