If Barclays buys a stake in Absa it will be one of the largest foreign investments in South Africa in recent years. Absa and Barclays talks continueSouth Africa biggest retail bank Absa has said it is still in talks with UK bank Barclays over the sale of majority stake in the group. "Discussions with Barclays are continuing, but shareholders are advised that no agreement has been reached as to any offer being made by Barclays to acquire a majority stake in Absa," Absa said in a statement. Absa also announced that it expects to see earnings grow by 20-25% in its current financial year.
