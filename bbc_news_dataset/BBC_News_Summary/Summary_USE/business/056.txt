BMW sold nearly 95,000 cars in Asia last year, up 2.6% on 2003. The luxury carmaker saw strong sales of its three marques - BMW, Mini and Rolls-Royce - in Asia last year after the launch of three new models. The German carmaker is aiming to boost annual sales in Asia to 150,000 by 2008. BMW drives record sales in AsiaBMW has forecast sales growth of at least 10% in Asia this year after registering record sales there in 2004. BMW sold more than 100 of the iconic models compared with just ten the previous year.
