Burren awarded Egyptian contractsBritish energy firm Burren Energy has been awarded two potentially lucrative oil exploration contracts in Egypt. This latest contract expands Burren Energy's global exploration and production portfolio - it also holds contracts in Turkmenistan and the Republic of Congo. The company successfully bid for the two contracts, granted by government owned oil firms, covering onshore and offshore areas in the Gulf of Suez. Burren Energy already has a presence in Egypt, having been awarded an exploration contract last year.
