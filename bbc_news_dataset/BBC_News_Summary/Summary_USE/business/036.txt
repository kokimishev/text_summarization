US gives foreign firms extra timeForeign firms have been given an extra year to meet tough new corporate governance regulations imposed by the US stock market watchdog. Big US firms already have to meet the requirements, but smaller ones and foreign-based firms which list their shares on US stock markets originally had until the middle of this year.
